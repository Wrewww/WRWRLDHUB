Who we are?

Founded in 2010 by automotive enthusiasts, Pegasus Lifestyle Management began with a simple mission: to provide premium storage solutions for valuable vehicles that owners could trust implicitly.

What started as a single 10,000 square foot facility has grown into a network of premium storage locations across the country, serving collectors, enthusiasts, and professional clients alike.

Our team brings together decades of experience in automotive care, marine services, and facility management to create the perfect environment for your vehicles.
