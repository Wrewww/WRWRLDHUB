Our Story

Discover the passion and expertise behind Pegasus Lifestyle Management

Who we are?

Founded in 2010 by automotive enthusiasts, Pegasus Lifestyle Management began with a simple mission: to provide premium storage solutions for valuable vehicles that owners could trust implicitly. What started as a single 10,000 square foot facility has grown into a network of premium storage locations across the country, serving collectors, enthusiasts, and professional clients alike. Our team brings together decades of experience in automotive care, marine services, and facility management to create the perfect environment for your vehicles.

Our mission & values

At Pegasus, we're committed to more than just storing vehicles - we preserve passions and protect investments through our core values:

Excellence

We set the highest standards in vehicle care and facility management, constantly innovating to serve our clients better.

Integrity

We operate with complete transparency and honesty, building relationships based on trust and reliability.

Passion

Our team shares our clients' enthusiasm for fine vehicles, bringing care and attention to every detail.

Security

We provide peace of mind through state-of-the-art security systems and rigorous protocols.
