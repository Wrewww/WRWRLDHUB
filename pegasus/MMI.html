Bringing Destroyed Vehicles Back to Life

When a vehicle has suffered catastrophic damage—whether from accidents, natural disasters, or combat conditions—our specialized restoration team works alongside MMI engineers to meticulously rebuild it to original (or better-than-original) condition. We handle everything from structural reconstruction to full mechanical and cosmetic restoration.

Our collaboration with the Mors Mutual Insurance (MMI) gives us access to:

Battlefield-proven repair techniques

Multi-grade materials and components

Advanced structural analysis technology

Precision engineering expertise

Our Comprehensive Restoration Process

Damage Assessment & Engineering Analysis
Our team conducts a complete forensic examination of the vehicle using:

  • 3D laser scanning for structural deformation analysis
  • MMI's proprietary damage assessment protocols
  • Computerized stress point modeling

    • Structural Reconstruction
    Military-inspired frame repair techniques including:

  • Jig-based chassis straightening
  • Laser-aligned frame measurement
  • Armor-grade welding and reinforcement

    • Mechanical & Systems Restoration
    Complete drivetrain and system rebuilding featuring:

  • MMI-certified engine rebuilding
  • Military-spec wiring harness replacement
  • Hydraulic/pneumatic system overhaul

    • Bodywork & Finish
    Precision body restoration with:

  • Aerospace-grade metal forming
  • Ballistic composite panel fabrication
  • Show-quality paint and finish

    • Testing & Certification
    Rigorous validation process including:

  • Dyno testing and systems diagnostics
  • Dyno testing and systems diagnostics
  • 500-mile road test protocol
